Why Coding for Kids?

Starting early is key. Many successful innovators—like Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg, and Elon Musk—began coding at a young age, and so did Adi. However, our goal isn’t to push children into programming careers but to nurture problem-solving and critical thinking skills. Coding helps kids think creatively and analytically, as Steve Jobs once said.

These skills will prepare young learners to handle bigger challenges ahead. In fact, the World Economic Forum lists analysis and critical thinking among the top skills for the future.

Program Motivation

After Adi’s coding video went viral, parents requested similar coding opportunities for their children. So, we created this course to meet that demand and to help kids unlock new skills, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning.
